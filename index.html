Features

Material Design 3

Built strictly following Material You. Fluid shapes, adaptive colors, and zero harsh shadows or neon effects.

Smart Search

Quickly toggle between different search engines directly from your home screen with a single click.

Your Shortcuts

Organize your favorite sites. The system features built-in native icons for major tech ecosystems.

Weather Forecast

Minimalist embedded weather widget displaying the current forecast with clean, fluid SVGs.
