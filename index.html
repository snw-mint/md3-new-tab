The Main Features

Smart Widgets & Search Stay informed with real-time weather forecasts, custom greetings, date/time clocks, and a smart search bar with quick-toggle engines.
Total Layout Control You decide what is displayed. Disable any elements you don't need for a fully clean setup, and easily import or export your settings.
Shortcuts & Launcher Organize up to 40 shortcuts in a flexible 4x10 grid with easy customization, and access productivity links from the integrated launcher.
